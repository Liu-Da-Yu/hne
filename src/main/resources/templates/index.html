About Haonuo

Zibo Haonuo Electronics Co.,Ltd is a professional lithium ion battery supplier, based in Zibo, Shandong province, founded by an aspiring team that working in lithium ion battery fields many years.

Our focus is lithium ion batteries. especially LiFePO4 battery, why lithium ion batteries? Because they are the best energy storage option in terms of cost, safety, and feasibility. the energy could be widely used in Home&Industry EES, Telecom backup power, UPS, EV, Fishing boat, Solar, ect fields.

Haonuo Electronics, working with famous cell mft like Samsung, LG, Goldencell, CATL, BAK, etc, supplys standard lithium ion battery packs for use in a wide variety of applications and produces custom packs for specific projects and OEM projects.

Our goal is to enable the widespread deployment of this cutting-edge technology in order to make green, renewable energy available for everyone.
